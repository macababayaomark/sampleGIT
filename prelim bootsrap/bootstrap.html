About Me

Hello! I'm Mark Ruez L. Macababayao, a passionate developer and photographer with a love for creativity and problem-solving. I find joy in developing software that helps people and expressing my artistic side through photography. At the age of 22, I am constantly learning and growing, both in my technical skills and in my ability to capture the beauty of the world around me through the lens. Born on December 18, 2002, I've always been curious about technology and art, and I aim to combine both of these passions to create meaningful work.

Whether I'm coding a new project or capturing a moment in time, I'm driven by the idea that both development and photography have the power to tell powerful stories and shape experiences. I'm excited to share my journey with you and hope that my work resonates with you!
